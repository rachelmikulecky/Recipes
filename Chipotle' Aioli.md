# Chipotle' Aioli
active: 5
inactive: 
source: Hawaiian cooking class
## Ingredients
* 1 chipotle in adobo
* 1 1/2 cup mayo
* 2 roughly chopped green onions (green part only)
* 2 garlic cloves, minced
* 3 tbsp lemon juice
* 3/4 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp paprika
* 1/4 tsp ground black peper
## Directions
1. Blend in a mini food processor or blender until smooth.
